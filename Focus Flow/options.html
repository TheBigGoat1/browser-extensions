FOCUS FLOW

Advanced Settings & Information
About

Focus Flow helps you stay focused on YouTube by removing distracting elements. Use the extension popup to toggle features on and off.

Reset Settings
This will reset all settings to their default values. You'll need to refresh YouTube pages for changes to take effect.
Keyboard Shortcuts

Available shortcuts (customize at chrome://extensions/shortcuts):

  • Ctrl+Shift+F (Mac: Cmd+Shift+F) - Toggle extension on/off
  • Ctrl+Shift+S (Mac: Cmd+Shift+S) - Toggle sidebar hiding
  • Ctrl+Shift+C (Mac: Cmd+Shift+C) - Toggle comments hiding
Channel Management

Use the extension popup to manage whitelisted and blacklisted channels. Whitelisted channels will always show all content, while blacklisted channels will always hide distractions.

Analytics

Focus Flow tracks usage statistics locally on your device for improvement purposes. No data is sent to external servers. All analytics are stored in your browser's local storage.
